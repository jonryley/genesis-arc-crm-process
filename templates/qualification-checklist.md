# Qualification Checklist

## Is it real
- Is there a clear problem or opportunity
- Is there a credible trigger
- Is there buyer engagement

## Is it worth it
- Is the value meaningful
- Is it aligned to strategy
- Is it worth internal effort

## Can we win it
- Do we have access
- Do we have relevance
- Do we have differentiation

## Can we deliver it
- Do we have capability
- Do we have capacity
- Do we understand the risk
